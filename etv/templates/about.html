    VISION
    Restore Prosperity, Peace and Power to ~14% of the US population - the Black community (47 Million+ Americans)
    MISSION
    Empower The Village, Inc. (ETV) is a data-driven nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization founded in 2018 to develop, guide and implement breakthrough, scalable strategies that empower Black people, businesses and community organizations to realize their full potential.
    STRATEGIC IMPERATIVE
    Apply our Strategic Partnerships Service Delivery Model to accomplish our goals while empowering and amplifying the impact of existing Black-led, as well as other organizations and individuals working to make significant advancements within the Black community

    Founder, CEO & President

    AYO SANDERSON WILSON

    The time for simply “admiring” problems, discussing root causes, and being satisfied with modest, incremental change for Black Americans has passed, according to Ayo Sanderson Wilson.

    That’s why she has transitioned from corporate America to make Empower The Village (ETV) a full-time commitment. Sanderson Wilson recently transitioned from ETV board chair to serve as the Chief Executive Officer and President of the data-driven 501(c)(3) organization. She founded ETV in 2018 to restore Prosperity, Peace and Power to the Black community by strategically partnering with “Village” leaders and allies to empower Black people, businesses, and community organizations to realize their full potential.

    Executive Director

    CHERYL R. EASLEY

    The time for simply “admiring” problems, discussing root causes, and being satisfied with modest, incremental change for Black Americans has passed, according to Ayo Sanderson Wilson.

    Cheryl Easley has been affiliated with Empower The Village, Inc. since its 2018 inception and serves as Executive Director. She brings over 30 years of experience in advertising, consumer packaged goods (CPG) marketing, and consulting for start-ups, small businesses, non-profit organizations and political campaigns. Cheryl obtained marketing management experience with Fortune 500 entities including Gannett, Sara Lee-Hanes, Bausch and Lomb and Dow Brands. She is a published writer of web content, fundraising solicitations, and marketing materials, as well as an accomplished presenter and public speaker.
