ETV’s Black Friday (BF) Challenges are friendly competitions designed to help all consumers recirculate dollars & increase economic prosperity in the Black community!

Individuals, corporations, organizations, and other groups can participate in a challenge with fun prizes!

The Real Winners are the businesses supported and communities that prosper as a result of the BF Challenge!
